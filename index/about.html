About SightSee

How many of you have traveled to Agra? I would presume many. Based on this, I can almost guarantee every one of you has seen the Taj Mahal while traveling to Agra. However, I bet not many of you can tell me about the streets of Agra or your experience seeing the Chini Ka Rauza monument in Agra. Travel is about the journey and not about the destination, while a universally beloved statement, I think travel is also about the kind of journey that you experience. Therefore, to give you a new traveling experience, I am proud to announce my latest project, #SightSee.com. SightSee is a domestic travel website to enhance the locality element in your traveling stories. SightSee enables you to search any location and look into the local locations and hidden gems of each location for you to go and wander to unknown territories accompanied by uncertain yet exciting memories.


Speaking of uncertainty, if you are not sure about a particular locality, SightSee gives you a list of the merits and demerits of each locality. Furthermore, SightSee is the pioneer in enabling its users to book certified personal travel guides. So now you can be guided to the adventure.


SightSee is a niche component of modern-day travel to provide a localized exploration with the simplicity and ease of any other modern-day traveling website. Go ahead and please comment your thoughts and visit our website SightSee.com and #SeeItForYourself.
